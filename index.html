Our Theme

"Tired of making a way through the traffic of tons of vehicles emitting poisonous gases and making honking noises of horns?
ZEST is here with its newest iteration of Cyclothon, " CYCLOTHON - Gear to Go Green " to soothe your soul, by attempting to promote a cleaner, greener and better environment for a better future by inculcating Cycling Culture in society.
Zest'23 CYCLOTHON appeals to you to join us and pedal for a safer and greener tomorrow!!"
" CYCLOTHON - Gear to Go Green " aims to take an initiative for creating a cleaner and greener environment by reducing carbon footprints on an individual level and by instilling a cycling culture in society that respects, embraces, and promotes Cyclists.
ZEST'23 Cyclothon appeals to everyone to accept cycling as an alternative for fuel vehicles to Go Green ...!

Categories

15 Km - Open For All

Route

Cyclothon will start from COEP hostel ground to SPPU University chowk and further through Ganesh Khind road from Rishi chowk and continue on Aundh Wakad road to cross the Rajiv Gandhi bridge on Mula river. Taking left from chowk before Sangavi traffic police station continues on Mahadji Shinde road and heads towards Westend mall. Taking the next left after Western mall continues on DP road to meet at Rishi chowk, taking right from Rishi chowk continue on Ganesh Khind Road to COEP hostel.
Registration Fees - 15 KM Category - ₹ 499 (Early Bird Offer- ₹ 419)

30 Km - Open For All

Route

Cyclothon will start from COEP hostel ground and proceed through JM to SPPU University chowk and further through Ganesh Khind road crossing Bal Kalyan Sanstha from Rishi chowk and continue on the Aundh-Wakad road to cross Rajiv Gandhi bridge on Mula river. Continue on Aundh-Wakad road toward Delhi Darbar dining hall and enter Wakad and meet at Wakad -Bhosari road. After crossing the Wakad police station, take a right from the azzuro-wakad road. meet at Baner road after crossing Babasaheb Ambedkar bridge through the PMPML bus stop. From Baner road reach SPPU University chowk and further the COEP hostel.
Registration Fees - 30 KM Category - ₹ 699 (Early Bird Offer- ₹ 599)
